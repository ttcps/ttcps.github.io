COVID-19

COVID-19 (Conoravirus disease 2019) is an infectious disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2, also known as COVID-19 Virus). Untill June 27 2020, it made overs 654,327 human died.

To in order to prevent COVID-19, you should take the solutions below:

    　
  • Stay home
    • 　
  • Wear an effective surgical mask
  • Social distancing(1.5m or above)
  • Wash hands always
  • Cover your mouth when you cough
  • See an doctor when you sick, remember to wear mask
